How we calculated this
  • Location = country baseline × region factor × urbanity (public price-level indices). US: BEA RPP; UK: ONS regional price levels.
  • Board suggestions are ranges adjusted by that factor (DE: full livery often €300–€400; up to ~€600 near big cities).
  • Hay/month (kg) ≈ horseKg × (forage%/100) × 30; cost via bale weight & price.
  • Farrier/month ≈ (cost/visit) × (12 ÷ (weeks/4.345)).
  • Vet & Dental/month = annual/12. Educational estimates; confirm locally.
